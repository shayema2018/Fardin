ABOUT US

Fardin is one of the most popular foreign admission consulting farm in Bangladesh. We guide you and your friends throughout your success.

Incepted in 2016, our core activity is to provide comprehensive career counselling for a better understanding of the educational scenario and career prospects overseas and to assist our pupils in making the right choice while pursuing these. Fardin is the official associate of several foreign Universities and is the largest of its kind in Bangladesh for sending students to countries like USA, Canada, UK, New Zealand, Europe, Central America, Singapore, Malaysia, China & Philippines, Poland, Lithuania, Latvia and more Europian countries.

Specially, we offer various scholarship for china universities. In china we arrange scholarship, low cost tuition fees/Stipend and many more ....

Fardin remain unrivalled in our spheres of activity. Our 100% visa success rate itself makes us beyond all competitions. We have sent more than 2000 students every year for higher education in countries across the globe for a variety of courses. We have associations with a large number of universities spread across 17 countries and Farzin has administrative offices in all of them. Apart from this, we have 21 marketing offices in India.

Considering the recent paradigm shift witnessed in the academic front across the country and the whooping demand on higher education, India is looking towards the Private Sector and Foreign Universities to bridge the projected gap. FARDIN is the only solution-centric consultant offering tailor-made career paths to suit individual profiles within individual constraints-educational, financial or personal.

We educate aspiring candidates regarding International Education assisting them to choose the right course at the right university. This is followed by a professional admission guidance and expert visa assistance. We offer students time-tested and cost effective process that enables a quick admission and smooth immigration process. We make our students feel at home providing all necessary aid to them. Some of our main features include pick up and drop from the airport, and providing any kind of help during your initial "settling-in" period. Above all, our officials visit universities at least thrice a year.

The Fardin Team strictly abide by a code of ethics and are bestowed with a wealth of experience thereby offering the most innovative and time-saving admission information that will make the otherwise laborious application processes a simple affair. All of our staff have firsthand experience and knowledge of education abroad and can easily steer anyone to their study destination and course.

To further make your process hassle free, we are located in highly prominent and easily accessible locations across the nation, which is yet another feather in our cap, making us the undisputed leader in the arena.
